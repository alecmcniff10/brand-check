About the editor

Alec McNiff

I’m an attorney at a large, national law firm. My practice focuses on brand protection and intellectual property law, including trademark, copyright, and advertising matters. While my day-to-day work spans a variety of industries, I’m especially interested in how brand protection law plays out in sports, entertainment, and the broader creator economy. This blog is where I explore that intersection by diving into NIL and licensing deals, trademark battles over team names and logos, copyright issues in music and film, advertising controversies, and other related developments. If you’re a brand owner, a rights holder, or just someone who follows the business side of sports and entertainment, I hope you find something useful here.
